# Home Builder 5

Home Builder is a free, open-source [Blender](https://www.blender.org/) add-on for designing interior spaces — kitchens, baths, and closets. It provides tools for drawing rooms, placing cabinets with intelligent wall snapping, and generating technical drawings.

## What You Can Do

- **Draw rooms** with configurable walls (exterior, interior, half walls), floors, and ceilings
- **Place cabinets & Appliances** — base, upper, tall, and appliances snap to walls and fill areas in a logical way
- **Configure everything** through right-click prompts: adjust cabinet dimensions, interior configurations, and construction
- **Place doors and windows** that automatically cut wall openings
- **Create layout views** — wall elevations, floor plans, and multi-view sheets with dimensions and annotations
